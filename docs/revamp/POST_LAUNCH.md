## 13.4 Lead Generation Scaling
- Week 1-2: Personal network
- Week 3-4: LinkedIn prospecting
- Week 5-6: Partner outreach
- Week 7-8: Facebook groups
- Week 9-10: Cold email (200 med spas)
    - **Compliance Requirements**:
    - Include valid physical postal address (CAN-SPAM).
    - Provide clear, functioning opt-out/unsubscribe mechanism (must be honored within 10 business days).
    - Accurately identify sender in "From" field and use honest subject lines (clear identification of commercial content required).
    - Check suppression lists before sending (internal do-not-contact lists + partner lists).
    - GDPR/CASL: Require prior opt-in or legitimate interest assessment; generally prohibit cold email to EU/Canada (consult counsel).
    - **QA Step**: Test unsubscribe processing and legal review for GDPR/CASL before campaign launch.
- Week 11-12: Google Ads ramp-up
